Improved Glance Tab

by ruff_hi


With version 3.13 of Civ4:BtS, the 'Glance Tab' within the Foreign Advisor Screen was removed for "being to ugly.".
We felt the information given in this screen was vital, and so returned it to the Foreign Advisor screen, but improved. We realigned the grid, as well as adding the attitude icons, and color coding the modifiers. It should now be easier to see where you stand with your rivals.


Instructions

The 'Glance Tab' can be enabled in the Advisors tab of the BUG Options Screen (Alt+Ctrl+O), by simple selection of the 'Glance Tab' checkbox.

To access it in game, the player simply has to open the Foreign Advisor Screen an then click on 'Glance' tab.
